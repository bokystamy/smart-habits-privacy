Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Smart Habits Pro collects the following information:

  • Personal profile data: name, age, height, weight, target weight, gender, fitness goals, experience level.
  • Optional profile photo stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data: app interactions, in-app preferences, settings, and AI coaching choices.
  • Device data: device model, operating system version, network status.
  • Workout tracking data: location (if enabled), exercise type, duration, and intensity.
  • Advertising data: advertising ID and related information for personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized fitness and AI coaching features.
  • To maintain and restore your profile and app settings.
  • To display ads through Google AdMob, if you have not purchased the Premium version.
  • To process in-app purchases for Premium features.
  • To improve app functionality, performance, and user experience.

3. Data Storage

All personal profile data, workout history, and profile images are stored locally on your device. Your data is not shared with third parties except for advertising services if enabled.

4. Advertising

Smart Habits Pro uses Google AdMob to display ads. Advertising services may collect non-personal information, such as device identifiers, device type, operating system version, and app usage patterns. Ads can be removed by purchasing the Premium version of the app.

5. Consent, GDPR & US State Privacy Laws

Smart Habits Pro uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage user consent where required by applicable privacy laws.

European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom & Switzerland

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we request your consent in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive before displaying personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

United States – State Privacy Laws

For users located in certain U.S. states (including, but not limited to, California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, and Utah), we comply with applicable state privacy laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). Where required, we provide notice and the ability to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data for advertising purposes.

Your consent choice is stored and respected by Google’s advertising services and automatically applied to ads shown within the app.

6. Permissions

  • Camera – for optional profile photo.
  • Location – for tracking workouts and routes.
  • Internet – for ads and optional cloud backup/restore.
  • Notifications – for reminders and coaching notifications.
  • Foreground Service – for tracking during active workouts, including Wake Lock.
  • Microphone – optional, for AI exercise guidance or voice commands.
  • Alarms – to schedule workout reminders.
  • Vibration – for notifications and reminders.
  • Billing – for Premium purchases within the app.
  • License Check – to verify legitimate use of the app.
  • Advertising ID and related permissions – for displaying ads.
  • Additional system permissions – required for proper app functionality.

7. Hardware Features

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Touchscreen

8. Your Choices

  • You can purchase the Premium version to remove ads.
  • You can delete your profile or reset the app to remove all data stored on your device.
  • You can disable app permissions (location, camera, microphone, notifications) in your device settings.
  • Profile photos and workout history are stored locally; deleting the app removes this data.
  • You can change or withdraw your advertising consent at any time in the app under Settings → Privacy choices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
